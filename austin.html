The City of Austin

    • Incorporated on December 27, 1839, it is the 11th-most populous city in the United States,[7] the fourth-most-populous city in Texas, the second-most-populous state capital city, one of two state capitals with a population of over one million people,[8] after Phoenix, Arizona,[9][10] and the most populous state capital that is not also the most populous city in its state.[9] It has been one of the fastest growing large cities in the United States since 2010.[11][12][13] Downtown Austin and Downtown San Antonio are approximately 80 miles (129 km) apart, and both fall along the Interstate 35 corridor. Some observers believe that the two regions may some day form a new "metroplex" similar to austin and Fort Worth.[14][15] Austin is the southernmost state capital in the contiguous United States and is considered a "Beta" global city as categorized by the Globalization and World Cities Research Network.
